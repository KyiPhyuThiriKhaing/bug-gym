## Question 6
Read two integers `a b` and print all numbers in the range that are Armstrong numbers.

An Armstrong number equals the sum of its digits each raised to the power of the number of digits.

Print in increasing order separated by spaces.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|1 500|1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 153 370 371 407|
|200 250||
