## Question 5
Read `R C` and an `R x C` matrix. Print the coordinates of the first saddle point found.

A saddle point is the smallest in its row and largest in its column.

Output format: `row col` (0-based). If none exists, print `-1 -1`.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|3 3 <br> 9 8 7 <br> 5 3 2 <br> 6 6 7|0 2|
|2 2 <br> 1 2 <br> 3 4|-1 -1|
