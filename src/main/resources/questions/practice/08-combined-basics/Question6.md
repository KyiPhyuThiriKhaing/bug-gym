## Question 6
Given N integers, print the second largest **distinct** value.

The input starts with N, followed by N integers.

You may assume there are at least two distinct values.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|6 <br> 5 1 9 9 3 7|7|
|5 <br> -1 -5 -2 -1 -3|-2|
