## Question 7
Read N integers and rotate the array to the right by K positions.

Input format:
- First line: `N K`
- Second line: `N` integers

Print the rotated array as space-separated values.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|5 2 <br> 1 2 3 4 5|4 5 1 2 3|
|4 5 <br> 9 8 7 6|6 9 8 7|
